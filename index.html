Do you simply looking for a cool planning tool you can use in your startup? Or do you just want to organize your day with a todo list?

We currently run here a crowdfunding/marketing page for an entire new concept of planning. Without the need, but of corse the possiblity, of a central server giving you still the option working together with others in a team. Of corse with high focus on usability and mobile support

Our goal is to get at least 1000 followers/tweets/likes before we start to ensure there's a community out there that likes the idea and use the tool. Just follow us on twitter to stay up to date.

Imagine the alternatives, for planning today you simply grab a tool on the web or download an app. But what happens if you want to share you tasks with someone, or you want to put personal information in?

Or think of you're working in an enterprise company that has restrictions regarding the installation of tools on your device. And even the usage of cloud or internet tools is prohibited because the data must not be stored outside of the company.

Therefore a new decentral planning tool should close that gap. HTML5 capabilities enable rich web application that store data local without the need of installing anything. In the tool should work on a mobile device in the same way as in a browser. To still be able to work in a team a decentral synchronization approach enables the application to sync with other instances/browsers. In a future version this can also be used to sync a larger amount of data with a sever.
